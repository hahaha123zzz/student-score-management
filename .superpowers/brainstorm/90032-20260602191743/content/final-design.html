📋 OOP 类设计（6个核心类）

User (基类)

user_id, name, password, role
+ login(), changePassword()

Student

student_id, name, class_id, grade
+ getInfo(), updateInfo()

Exam

exam_id, name, subjects[], weights[]
+ createExam(), publish()

Grade

student_id, exam_id, scores{}
+ calcTotal(), rank()

⚙️ 编译方式（不用CMake）

直接用 g++ 一行编译
g++ -std=c++17 -I include -I lib -o edugrade.exe ^
  src/main.cpp src/util.cpp src/auth.cpp ^
  src/student.cpp src/exam.cpp ^
  src/grade.cpp src/stats.cpp ^
  -lws2_32
